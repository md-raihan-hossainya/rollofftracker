About SISCOTEK and the CTS Mission

We are SISCOTEK, a technology firm dedicated to building powerful, industry-specific solutions that turn operational pain points into competitive advantages. The Container Tracking System (CTS) is the result of focused development tailored specifically for the roll-off and container rental industry.

SISCOTEK Team/Mission Image Placeholder

Our Mission: Total Control & Profitability

We understand that in the roll-off industry, every minute counts, every container is capital, and compliance is non-negotiable. Our mission is to provide an intuitive, reliable system that gives owners, dispatchers, and drivers **real-time transparency** and the automation tools needed to eliminate waste and maximize profitability on every route.

CTS is actively developed with the input of industry experts to ensure it meets the unique demands of container rental, including intricate DOT requirements and complex QuickBooks synchronization.
