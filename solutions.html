Solving the Toughest Roll-Off Challenges

The Container Tracking System (CTS) was built from the ground up to eliminate the three most costly problems in container rental and waste hauling: operational inefficiency, inaccurate billing, and compliance risk.

Eliminate Wasted Miles: Route Optimization

**Challenge:** Manual routing leads to wasted time, fuel costs, and driver confusion, directly eating into your profit margins.

The CTS Solution: Intelligent Dispatching

  • ✓ **Auto-Sequencing:** Automatically calculate the shortest, most efficient route based on real-time traffic and operational constraints.
  • ✓ **Mobile Dispatch:** Send routes and job details directly to the driver's phone, minimizing communication errors.
  • ✓ **Fuel Savings:** Reduce route time and distance by up to 20%.

Stop Revenue Leakage: Accurate Billing

**Challenge:** Overages, misapplied payments, and double data entry between dispatch and accounting slow down cash flow and introduce risk.

The CTS Solution: QuickBooks Automation

  • ✓ **Auto-Invoicing:** Generate precise invoices in minutes, capturing every billable charge (overages, tonnage) straight from the closed ticket.
  • ✓ **Accounting Sync:** Instant synchronization of all transactional data with QuickBooks to ensure accurate financial reporting.
  • ✓ **PO Management:** Validate services against client Purchase Orders (POs) before billing.

Mitigate Risk: Asset & Compliance Control

**Challenge:** Losing track of expensive containers or dealing with expired driver licenses and overdue DOT permits creates massive liability.

The CTS Solution: Proactive Visibility

  • ✓ **GPS Tracking:** Real-time location and status of every container (**CONTAINERS**), reducing asset loss and improving utilization.
  • ✓ **Automated Reminders:** System notifies you of upcoming deadlines for **DOT PERMITS**, truck maintenance, and driver licenses.
  • ✓ **Audit Ready:** Maintains a detailed **Audit Trail** of all operational changes and compliance documents.
