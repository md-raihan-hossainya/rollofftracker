Feature Page: Billing & Accounting

**Field-to-Invoice Automation.** Automatically generate precise invoices based on completed tickets, handle overage charges, and instantly sync with **QuickBooks**.

  • **Seamless QuickBooks Interface:** Instantly synchronize all invoices, payments, and customer records. Eliminate manual data entry and errors.
  • **Accurate Invoicing & Quotation:** Capture tonnage and overage charges automatically from completed **TICKETS**, ensuring you bill for every service.
  • **PO Management:** Track and manage customer-issued Purchase Orders against jobs to validate billing and prevent revenue loss.

Feature Page: Asset Tracking & Maintenance

**Never Lose a Container. Ever.** Real-time asset tracking with maintenance logging ensures every container and truck is utilized and legal.

  • **Real-Time Inventory Control:** Track every **CONTAINER** by status (Available, Active, In-Transit) and precise location to improve utilization and drive revenue.
  • **Proactive Maintenance:** Schedule and track all vehicle and asset **MAINTENANCE** for Drivers, Trucks, and other assets to maximize uptime and fleet safety.
  • **Disposal Site Management:** Maintain accurate records for Transporters and Disposal Sites for seamless routing and waste tracking.

Feature Page: DOT Permits & Compliance

**Compliance Guarantee.** Automatically track municipal permits, DOT expirations, and regulatory documentation to avoid costly fines and service delays.

  • **Mandatory Permit Tracking:** Link permits (**DOT PERMITS**) directly to job sites, ensuring every drop is legal before the truck leaves the yard.
  • **Expiration Alerts:** Automatically flag permits that are **Required & Expiring** soon (including driver licenses and truck registrations) using the maintenance module.
  • **Valid & Expired Tracking:** Maintain a clear list of **All D.O.T Permits** and separate **Valid & Expired DOT** records for rapid auditing.

CTS Quick Start Guide

The Container Tracking System (CTS) gives you total control. Master these core processes in minutes to streamline your operation from Day One.

1. Order Creation

**Goal:** Create a complete, billable work order in under 60 seconds.

  • Select Customer, Job Type (Drop, Switch, Pickup).
  • System checks inventory and DOT permit needs automatically.
  • Enter Quotation and PO Number to finalize.

2. Route Assignment

**Goal:** Build the most profitable route for the day's load.

  • Use the interactive Map Board to see all open tickets.
  • Drag-and-Drop tickets onto a driver for automatic sequence optimization.
  • Driver updates status (e.g., "Container Dropped") via mobile, closing the office loop.

3. Financial Sync

**Goal:** Ensure every completed job is accurately billed and synced.

  • Review tickets marked "Closed" in the Invoicing module.
  • System automatically calculates rental duration and overage charges.
  • Use the **QuickBooks Interface** to batch-send approved invoices.
